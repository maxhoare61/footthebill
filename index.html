We use complex data to tell clear stories
Because public data should be public-friendly

The Problem

Public data isn’t always publicly accessible. Key insights from government data are often
scattered across PDFs, spreadsheets, and fragmented databases.
While technically publicly available, understanding it requires expertise, and a lot of time.

our Approach

At Downstream, we believe public data should be truly public.
We transform dense datasets into clear, interactive visuals and scrollable narratives.
We handle the data cleaning, analysis, and visualisations - you explore the insights.

Current Projects

We're starting small but focusing on what matters — making carbon
emissions and political donation data accessible to everyone through
interactive storytelling. These are the stories we care about, and we believe
you should be able to understand them too.
