The Problem

Public data isn’t always publicly accessible. Key insights from government data are often scattered across spreadsheets and fragmented databases. While technically publicly available, understanding requires expertise, and a lot of time. Since this data helps voters decide on important issues, this is bad for our democracy.

Our Approach

At Downstream, we believe public data should be truly public. We transform dense datasets into clear, interactive visuals and scrollable narratives. We handle the data cleaning, analysis, and visualisations - you explore the insights.
